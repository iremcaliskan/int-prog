Historical Places Of Eskişehir

Sazova Park



A green heaven in the heart of the city, the Sazova Park is the perfect way to spend an afternoon at, if you are travelling with kids.


Highlights include: Medieval ship, Lake, Science Centre, Open Air theatre, Castle, Free Train


Soak up the sun while your little ones run around to explore the castle and the ship or make this a learning experience as you scan through the Science center. A must if you are looking for a relaxing place among green grass. The place also has a cafe- perfect for a light meal.


How To reach Sazova Park by Public Transport

A Tram line runs near the park.

Sazova Park Opening and Closing Hours

The nearby underwater centre closes at 7.00 pm.

Yazılıkaya



Popularly known as the Tomb of King Midas, Yazilikaya is a favourite attraction among tourists and locals alike. Excavated by the French Archaeological Institute immediately after the Second World War, the place is located 66 kilometres south of Eskisehir.


Highlights include: Monument with Phrygian inscriptions. Temple front with acroteria faced with terra cotta. Kirkgoz cave (Forty eye cave).


The whole excavated site takes almost 2 hours to explore. A must if you love history archaeology or simply want to have an Indiana Jones experience!


Yazılıkaya Information

Don’t forget to carry your own water and snacks.

Do not litter the place.

Wear sturdy shoes and comfortable clothes and sunscreen.

Rent a car to reach the place.

How To reach Yazılıkaya by Public Transport

Rent a car :).

Lüleltaşı Museum



Meerschaum, better known as Sepiolite, is a mineral which is often used to make elaborate smoke pipes. The soft white mineral has the perfect consistency to carve into for making intricate designs. Located at the heart of the old part of the city, the building sits perfectly amidst the charm. At the museum you will find sepioloite pipes which will make you wonder if they are in fact made from marble! Scroll through an array of them which showcase detailing like never before. If you don’t mind spending, gift the pipes to your friends!

You can buy Sepiolite pipes here.

How To reach Luleltaşı Museum by Public Transport

Hire a Taxi to reach the place.

Odunpazarı



If you want to walk back in time in Turkey and see it reflected in the houses which adorn the old city, walk through the Odunpazari- the old part of the city! Small streets lined with colourful houses give the place an old-world charm which can only be experienced by exploring its nooks and crannies. A must if you love to explore unique spaces of a city. You want to carry a camera for this one!


Do carry a camera to capture the unique houses.


Ask the locals to guide you to the ‘Old City’.


How To reach Odunpazarı by Public Transport

You can walk to reach the place.
